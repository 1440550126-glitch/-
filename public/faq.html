FAQ

Q1: What is LingMirror AI?

LingMirror AI is an AI content and video production engine that helps users generate scripts, product ads, marketing copy, images, videos and consistent multi-shot AI video projects.

Q2: How is it different from normal AI video tools?

Normal AI video tools generate one clip and forget the context. LingMirror AI builds project memory, extracts frames, tracks characters, scenes and props, and uses that memory before generating the next shot.

Q3: What is Memory Anchor?

Memory Anchor is the project memory system. It stores characters, props, scenes, costumes, frames, prompts and quality reports so future shots can stay consistent.

Q4: What is AI Director?

AI Director plans shots, checks consistency, detects visual problems and decides whether a shot should be regenerated.

Q5: Can I continue a project later?

Yes. LingMirror saves project memory snapshots. If a project stops, you can continue from the last approved shot.

Q6: What happens when my balance is not enough?

The project pauses safely. After recharge or subscription renewal, the system automatically resumes from the latest checkpoint.

Q7: Are credits crypto tokens?

No. LingMirror Credits are only internal AI generation credits. They are not cryptocurrency, not withdrawable, not transferable and not investment products.

Q8: Can I use it for product ads?

Yes. Product Ads Generator can create product selling points, ad copy, short video scripts and video plans.

Q9: Can businesses use this?

Yes. Businesses can create promo videos, product explainers, marketing copy, training scripts and knowledge base assistants.

Q10: Can developers use this?

Yes. Individual developers can use LingMirror to generate landing page content, app promo videos, product copy and AI content prototypes.

Q11: Does the system guarantee perfect videos?

No AI system can guarantee perfect output, but LingMirror improves consistency using Memory Anchor, quality checks and regeneration logic.

Q12: How do payments work?

Users recharge credits through PayPal or Alipay. AI tasks deduct credits automatically. Subscriptions add credits monthly.

Q13: Is subscription unlimited?

No. Subscription adds monthly wallet credits. AI tasks still deduct credits based on usage.

Q14: Can I create long videos?

Yes. The system creates multiple 4-15 second shots and automatically combines them. Longer videos require more credits.
